CARTOCANARY

Born in 1971, CartoCanary is the oldest member of the Greeks. He is known for his wisdom and guidance, often referred to by other members as "Unc", or "Maginot". He is the father of three Greek members including Caltr, SirC, and JenningsKisser92 who were all foundational in the creation of the Greeks. It is this fact that has earned CartoCanary the title of Notable Citizen for his contributions to the development and growth of the empire.

Early life as a GRΣΣK

CartoCanary became an offical member of the Greeks on April 27th, 2024, one day after the founding of the empire itself. He quickly became an integral member of the Greeks Esports team, dominating in Fornite.
